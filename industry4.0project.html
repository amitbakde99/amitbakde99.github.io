Project Duration

Conducted an end-to-end data-driven study to evaluate Industry 4.0 technology adoption among India’s leading companies listed in the NIFTY 200 index. Using advanced web scraping and text mining techniques, the project uncovered sector-specific trends in smart manufacturing, AI, IoT, and automation initiatives.

Problem Statement

While Industry 4.0 is reshaping global industries, a clear, data-backed understanding of its adoption in Indian companies remained limited. This project aimed to bridge that gap by analyzing publicly available digital footprints at scale.

Approach and Methodology

  • Developed a Python-based web scraping pipeline to extract data from 200 company websites, PDFs, and career portals.
  • Processed 50GB+ of unstructured text data using Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques including tokenization, lemmatization, and custom stopword removal.
  • Extracted frequency metrics of Industry 4.0-related keywords and built a co-occurrence matrix to map contextual relationships between terms.
  • Performed sector-wise comparative analysis across IT, healthcare, real estate, communication services, and consumer staples.
  • Visualized keyword networks to highlight technological maturity and gaps.

Tools and Technologies

  • Python: Scrapy, BeautifulSoup, Pandas, NetworkX, Matplotlib
  • Text Mining: NLP preprocessing, Frequency Analysis, Co-occurrence Networks
  • Visualization: Network Graphs, Sector-wise Heatmaps

Key Achievements

  • Collected and analyzed over 50GB+ of textual data across 56 companies.
  • Identified distinct Industry 4.0 adoption patterns across sectors — with IT and Communication sectors showing the highest maturity.
  • Created co-occurrence networks that visually mapped Industry 4.0 ecosystems within company communication.
  • Developed a reusable scraping and text analytics framework, adaptable for future large-scale research projects.

Personal Learning Outcomes

  • Mastered scalable web scraping techniques for heterogeneous web environments.
  • Deepened expertise in applying Natural Language Processing (NLP) for business research and strategic insights.
  • Strengthened skills in large-scale data processing, feature extraction, and sectoral comparative analysis.
  • Enhanced ability to communicate complex findings through compelling visual storytelling.
